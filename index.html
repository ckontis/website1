Roblox News

Roblox Studio is receiving several updates focusing on performance, usability, and new features. A major change involves the end of support for Windows 7, 8.1, and macOS 10.13 on March 20, 2025, requiring users to upgrade to Windows 10 or higher, or macOS 10.14 or higher. Additionally, Roblox is revamping the Asset Manager for improved asset management, search, and filtering, and introducing a new party API for more control over player parties within games.
